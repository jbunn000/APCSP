The Slider!

Made with Scratch

The Slider is a very simple game involving a sliding square controlled by the player. However, this is no ordinary sliding-block game; sometimes, the game will build itself to be too difficult, and in which case, the player can "reload" the level layout!
This game is still in its early stages and may continue to receive updates in the future.
